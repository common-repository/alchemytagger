AlchemyTagger analyzes your writing as you blog, suggesting useful tags
to describe and categorize your posts.

== Description ==

AlchemyTagger automatically works in the background as you're blogging,
analyzing your writing and suggesting useful tags for your posts. Tags 
make your posts easier to navigate, better-ranked by search engines, and more!

== Installation ==

To install this plugin into your WordPress blog, do the following:

1. Copy the "alchemytagger" directory into your WordPress plugins location:

    cp -R alchemytagger /path/to/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/

2. Login to your WordPress administration Dashboard.

3. Visit the "plugins" settings screen from your WordPress dashboard:

    a. Locate "AlchemyTagger" in your list of available plugins, and click "Activate".

    b. Click the "AlchemyTagger Configuration" tab from your plugins settings screen.

    c. Enter your AlchemyAPI key.  If you do not have one, a registration link is
       provided from the "AlchemyTagger Configuration" screen.

4. That's it!  Enjoy AlchemyTagger.

== Usage ==

AlchemyTagger automatically works in the background as you're blogging,
analyzing your writing and suggesting new tags for your posts.

You can choose to have AlchemyTagger work in conjunction with the default WordPress
tagging interface, or replace it entirely.

Tag suggestions may be retrieved automatically at any time, by clicking the 
"Suggest Tags" button from the AlchemyTagger interface.
